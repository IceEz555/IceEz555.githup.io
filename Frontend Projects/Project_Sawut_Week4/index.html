Introduction

The goal of this project is to express the passion and dedication of a football fan who wants to create a website about his favorite team as a personal tribute and lasting reference. The site is also intended for the new young generation who are deciding which football team to support, providing them with engaging information and an accessible introduction to the club. At the same time, it serves as a learning resource where visitors can explore and understand the history and background of the club.
