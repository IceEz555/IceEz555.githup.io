What is Fashion Week?

Fashion Week is a series of events where designers present their latest collections on the runway.
It's a major force in the fashion industry, setting trends and showcasing creative visions.

Global Fashion Events

Held in major cities (The big 4) New York, Paris, Milan, and London.

Trend Setting

Shapes upcoming styles and seasonal trends.

Industry Influence

Impacts media, culture, and the global market.

When Does It Happen?

Twice a year: February/March & September.

Why It Matters

Drives innovation and sets the stage for future trends.
